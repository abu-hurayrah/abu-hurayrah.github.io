Wahdah is a safe, secure and customisable terminal-based blogging platform with a client made in Java.

It achieves security and transparency with its account system and write-once-read-only database.

Account can be freely made without an email but can be deleted without warning by moderators, just like the blogs.

With the client being made in simple Java, customisations on its appearance can be made at the user's will.

The lack of required connections to other accounts means that you, fortunately, do not have to sell your soul to Google.

Though the client will be open-sourced, the database design and access system does not make it easy for databreaches to occur.

This allows for maximum transparency, privacy, customisability and security. Once something goes up, it never goes down!

This encourages freedom of speech and availability of information. However, fake news will be very common so use your judgment.

There will be moderators and if there is an account spreading misinformation, it and its posts will be taken down.
